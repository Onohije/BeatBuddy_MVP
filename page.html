About Us

Inspiration

Our music recommendation system was inspired by a shared passion for exploring new sounds and the joy that music brings to our lives. The idea was born from the desire to create a platform that goes beyond generic recommendations and truly understands the unique musical preferences of each listener. This project is a labor of love, fueled by our love for music and the belief that everyone deserves a personalized soundtrack.

This project is part of our journey at Holberton School, where we embrace the opportunity to apply our skills to real-world challenges and create meaningful solutions.
